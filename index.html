Limit Switches

These limit switches were designed to prevent the rover arm from exceeding a 360Â° rotation. The system uses three Hall sensors to detect magnetic field changes. When two of the sensors register the same reading, it signals that a rotation limit has been reached. Based on which pair of sensors triggers the limit, the arm adjusts its movement and rotates in the appropriate direction to stay within bounds.

Buck Converter

I am currently designing and building a buck converter with a focus on maximizing the switching frequency while minimizing the size of the PCB. My goal is to achieve high efficiency and stable output voltage in a small form factor, making it suitable for modern electronics applications. This project involves extensive testing and fine-tuning of components such as inductors, capacitors, and the control circuit to ensure reliable operation under varying loads. It is a hands-on exploration of power electronics and PCB design, further deepening my understanding of circuit efficiency and system optimization.
